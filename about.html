What is WTFood?

WTFood is a revolutionary AI-powered recipe generator that combines the practicality of cooking assistance with the entertainment of a sarcastic chef who isn't afraid to roast you while you roast your dinner.

The Problem We Solve

You stare into your fridge like it's a magic 8-ball, hoping dinner will materialize from the random assortment of ingredients. The same old "what should I cook?" dilemma that has plagued humanity since the invention of the refrigerator.

Traditional recipe apps are boring. They give you recipes, sure, but where's the personality? Where's the entertainment? Where's the chef who tells you that your ingredient choices are questionable?

That's where WTFood comes in. We don't just give you recipes - we give you an experience.
